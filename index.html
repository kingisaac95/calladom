About Calladom Solutions Ltd.

We are a software company based in Nigeria. We offer a wide range of services including but not limited to Software Development, Software Integration, Business Solutions Development, Web Application Development.

Founded in 2016, Calladom Solutions has been actively in the software manufacturing industry. We have developed and implemented various business software solutions since our inception.

Our standard of development and quality assuarance is second to none. We have successfully built a track record of wonderful software systems and an engaging after delivery suport. Our clients can fully attest to this, and we want that for you too.

Why us?

  • Dynamic bespoke solutions tailored to your specifications
  • Parallel software deployment
  • Engaging customer care service
  • Wonderful after sales support
